How to Use Chunker

1

Browse for CSV file

Click the 'Browse' button to select your large CSV file.

2

Adjust settings

Choose how you want to split your file (by rows or number of files).

3

Split CSV

Click 'Split' and watch the progress bar as your files are processed.

4

Find split files

Access your split files in the output directory you specified.
